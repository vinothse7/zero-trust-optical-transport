Abstract / Summary

This white paper, developed in collaboration between Ciena and Microsoft, explores how zero-trust optical design can deliver resilient metro connectivity. Microsoft contributes its tiered optical business continuity and disaster recovery (BCDR) architecture, while Ciena provides foundational transport technologies—the 6500 Packet-Optical Platform, 6500 Reconfigurable Line System (RLS), and the Waveserver® family.

Together, this approach integrates two autonomous optical domains into one logical service layer, enabling continuous, fail-safe operation that protects workloads and maintains uptime across the network edge.
